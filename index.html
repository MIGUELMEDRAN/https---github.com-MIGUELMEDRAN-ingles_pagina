Welcome to my Blog

Hello! My name is Cristian Lopez Anaya. I study Analysis and Software Development at SENA. This blog is part of my English learning process. I write simple texts about my life, work, and ideas. I hope you enjoy and learn with me!

1) Welcome to My Blog

This is my first post. I, Cristian Lopez Anaya, am happy to write in English. I want to improve step by step. I practice with short texts and clear ideas. I will talk about my studies, my job, my hobbies, and two important topics: Critical thinking and media literacy and Crime and punishment.

2) My Job in the Health Area

I, Cristian Lopez Anaya, started to work in the health area more than three years ago. I met many doctors and specialists. Some of them spoke to me in English. They used short phrases, and I tried to answer. It was a good experience. I learned to be calm and to listen first.

3) Exploring New Restaurants

I, Cristian Lopez Anaya, love visiting restaurants. When I can, I try a new place. I like to taste different food and learn new words.

4) Learning New Words Every Day

I, Cristian Lopez Anaya, like to read short phrases and new words. I use a small notebook and my phone. I write the word, a simple definition, and an example. I repeat the words many times during the week. This method is easy and useful for me.

5) Traveling Through Language

Sometimes I, Cristian Lopez Anaya, cannot travel, but English takes me to new places. When I read an article or watch a video in English, I feel like I am in another country. I hear different accents, new stories, and new perspectives.

6) Critical Thinking and Media Literacy

I, Cristian Lopez Anaya, think that today we read news and watch videos all the time. Some information is true. Some is false or confusing. Critical thinking means we stop and think before we believe or share.

7) Crime and Punishment

I, Cristian Lopez Anaya, believe that every society needs rules. When a person breaks the law, there is a consequence. The goal is not only to punish, but also to protect people and teach better behavior. A good system is fair and clear.
